Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Spartan Corporation Studio

What is Spartan Corporation Studio?

+

Spartan Corporation Studio is a Roblox game development studio founded in 2025. We specialize in creating immersive gaming experiences across multiple categories including Simulator, Lucky, Story, Horror, Roleplay, and Hangout games.

How can I contact Spartan Studio?

+

You can reach us through email at spctacularstudio@gmail.com, follow us on our social media channels (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube), or use the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours during business hours.

What are your business hours?

+

Our business hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (GMT+7). We provide limited support during weekends for urgent matters.

What games are currently in development?

+

We currently have three games in development: HangSim (a roleplay simulation game), Go Hunting (an animal hunting adventure), and Gunung Watu Welah (details coming soon). All games are currently in Pre-Alpha stage.

When will the games be released?

+

Our games are currently in Pre-Alpha development. We don't have specific release dates yet, but we'll announce them through our social media channels and website notifications as development progresses.

What is HangSim about?

+

HangSim is a classic roleplay game where you create and control a character following real-world rules. You can choose various careers like police officer, criminal, taxi driver, and many other jobs. The game features realistic systems and consequences for your actions.

What makes Go Hunting unique?

+

Go Hunting features multiple islands with different animal varieties. You can hunt, collect, and sell animals with three rarity tiers: Basicmals, Raremals, and Legendmals. Each animal type has different values and characteristics.

Do I need to create an account?

+

You don't need a separate account for our website. However, you'll need a Roblox account to play our games. Our website uses your Roblox username for notifications and updates about game releases.

Why do you need my Roblox username?

+

We collect your Roblox username to register you for our games and create your profile. This information is completely safe and will only be used to notify you when games are available and to manage your preferences. We never share this information with third parties.

How do I get notified about game updates?

+

You can get notifications through our website notification system, follow our social media accounts, or subscribe to our email updates. We'll announce major updates, release dates, and beta testing opportunities through these channels.

What platforms do your games support?

+

Our games are developed for Roblox, which supports PC, Mac, iOS, Android, and Xbox One. As long as you can access Roblox on your device, you'll be able to play our games.

How do I report bugs or issues?

+

You can report bugs through our contact form by selecting "Bug Report" as the subject, or email us directly at spctacularstudio@gmail.com. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue, including screenshots if applicable.

Will there be beta testing opportunities?

+

Yes! We plan to conduct beta testing for all our games before official release. Beta testers will be selected from our community and notified through our website and social media channels.
